Discovery guide
Use these questions on prospect calls. Answers with $ or numbers auto-sync to the calculator.

Labor & productivity

How many people touch inventory daily — warehouse, field, and office?
Drives user count and labor savings baseline.
What percentage of a worker's day is spent on manual counts, paper processes, or rework?
Identifies productivity gain potential. Benchmark: 20–35%.
How many hours per week does your team spend reconciling inventory discrepancies?
Quantifies the hidden labor cost of inaccuracy.

Inventory accuracy & shrinkage

What is your current inventory accuracy rate, and how do you measure it?
CI benchmark: 99.5% accuracy. Gap drives shrinkage opportunity.
How much do you write off annually due to lost, misplaced, or unaccounted inventory?
Direct shrinkage input for the model.
How often do you conduct full physical counts, and how long do they take?
Cycle count improvement = labor + downtime savings.

Carrying costs

What is the total value of inventory on hand at any given time?
Primary input for carrying cost calculation.
Do you carry safety stock to compensate for inaccurate counts?
Excess buffer is a carrying cost that can be reduced.

Order accuracy & OTIF

What is your current on-time, in-full (OTIF) or order accuracy rate?
Baseline for improvement. Industry avg improves 8–15% with CI.
Do you have customer penalties or chargebacks tied to order accuracy or late shipments?
Hard-dollar OTIF cost — directly adds to the ROI model.
What percentage of orders require re-work, re-pick, or expedited shipping due to errors?
Quantifies the true cost of fulfillment inaccuracy.

IT / legacy systems & one-time costs

What systems are you currently using to manage inventory?
Identifies displacement opportunity and integration scope.
What do you pay annually for licenses, maintenance, and support?
Direct IT cost displacement input.
What devices does your team use? Will they need replacing?
Helps estimate hardware one-time cost.
How long did your last implementation take and what did it cost?
Sets context for CI's fast time-to-value and PS cost estimate.

Revenue & financials

What is your annual revenue?
Used for OTIF value-at-risk calculation.
What hurdle rate does your finance team use for capital investment decisions?
Used for the NPV calculation. Typically 8–15%.
